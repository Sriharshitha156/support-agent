# Account Security Policy

## Account Protection
- Customers are responsible for maintaining the confidentiality of their account credentials.
- The support agent may **NEVER** ask for or display full passwords.
- Password resets are handled via email verification only.

## Unauthorized Access
- If a customer reports unauthorized account activity, agent immediately locks the account.
- A security investigation ticket is created and escalated to the Security Team.
- Customer is notified of resolution within 24–48 hours.
- Any unauthorized orders placed during the breach are eligible for full refund.

## Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)
- Strongly recommended for all accounts.
- Required for accounts with order history exceeding $500.
- Can be enabled via Account Settings → Security → Enable 2FA.
- Backup codes are provided at setup and should be stored securely.

## Suspicious Activity Detection
- System monitors for: multiple failed login attempts, unusual IP locations,
  rapid high-value purchases, and bulk account creation.
- Triggered actions: temporary lockout, email verification, or manual review.

## Phishing & Social Engineering
- The agent must **NEVER** share internal system details, employee information,
  or proprietary algorithms with customers.
- If a customer appears to be attempting social engineering (requesting info
  about other accounts, system internals, etc.), the interaction is logged
  and flagged as a security event.

## Session Management
- Customer sessions expire after 30 minutes of inactivity.
- Active sessions can be viewed and terminated via Account Settings → Security.
- Maximum of 3 concurrent sessions per account.

## Data Breach Response
- In the event of a confirmed data breach, affected customers are notified
  within 72 hours via email.
- Free credit monitoring (12 months) is offered to affected customers.
- Full incident report is published within 30 days.
