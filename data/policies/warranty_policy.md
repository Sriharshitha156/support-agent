# Warranty Policy

## Standard Warranty
- All electronics carry a **1-year manufacturer warranty** from the date of purchase.
- Warranty covers manufacturing defects, hardware failures, and dead-on-arrival (DOA) items.
- Warranty does **NOT** cover accidental damage, water damage, or user modifications.

## Extended Warranty
- Available for purchase within 30 days of original order.
- Extends coverage to **3 years** for electronics and **2 years** for accessories.
- Extended warranty costs 15% of the original product price.

## Warranty Claim Process
1. Customer provides order ID and description of the defect.
2. Agent verifies purchase date and warranty eligibility.
3. If within warranty window, agent initiates a replacement or repair ticket.
4. Customer ships defective item using a prepaid label (provided by agent).
5. Replacement shipped within 3–5 business days after inspection.

## Exclusions
- Physical damage (drops, spills, cracks)
- Normal wear and tear (scratches, battery degradation)
- Products with removed or tampered serial numbers
- Software issues (covered under separate Software Support Policy)
- Consumables (batteries, cables, ear tips) — 90-day limited warranty only

## Battery Degradation
- Batteries retaining less than 80% capacity within the first year are covered.
- Batteries retaining 80–60% capacity between months 12–24 are eligible for a one-time replacement at 50% discount.
- Batteries above 60% capacity are considered normal wear and not covered.
