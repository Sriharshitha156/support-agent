# Refund Policy

## Eligibility
- Refunds are available within **30 days** of delivery for most items.
- Digital goods are non-refundable once downloaded.
- Orders over **$500 USD** require human agent approval.

## Process
1. Customer provides order ID and reason for refund.
2. Agent verifies order status and eligibility.
3. Approved refunds are processed within 5–7 business days.

## Exceptions
- Defective items: full refund regardless of window (with photo evidence).
- Lost shipments: full refund after carrier investigation (7–14 days).
