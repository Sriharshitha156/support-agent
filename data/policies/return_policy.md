# Return & Exchange Policy

## Eligibility
- Items may be returned or exchanged within **30 days** of delivery.
- Items must be in original packaging with all accessories and tags attached.
- Opened software, digital downloads, and personal care items are **non-returnable**.

## Return Process
1. Customer initiates return request via chat or email.
2. Agent verifies order ID and return eligibility.
3. Agent generates a prepaid return shipping label.
4. Customer ships item within 14 days of label generation.
5. Refund or exchange processed within 5–7 business days of receiving the item.

## Exchange Process
- Exchanges are available for the same item in a different size, color, or variant.
- If the desired variant is out of stock, a full refund is issued instead.
- Price differences on exchanges: customer pays the difference if upgrading; store credit issued if downgrading.

## Condition Requirements
- **Like New**: Full refund. Item unused, original packaging intact.
- **Opened/Used**: 85% refund. Item shows minor use but no damage.
- **Damaged**: Case-by-case. If damage occurred during shipping, full refund. If customer-caused, up to 50% refund or store credit.

## Restocking Fee
- A **15% restocking fee** applies to electronics over $200.
- No restocking fee for items under $200 or for defective returns.

## Non-Returnable Items
- Custom or personalized products
- Gift cards and store credit
- Hazardous materials (batteries, chemicals)
- Perishable goods
- Items marked as "Final Sale" at time of purchase

## Late Returns
- Returns initiated after 30 days but within 60 days are eligible for **store credit only** (no cash refund).
- Returns after 60 days are not accepted unless covered under warranty.
