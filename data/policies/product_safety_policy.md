# Product Safety & Recall Policy

## Safety First
- If a customer reports a safety concern with a product (overheating, sparking,
  chemical odor, electrical shock), the agent **immediately** instructs the
  customer to stop using the product.
- Safety incidents are escalated to the Safety Team with HIGH priority.

## Product Recalls
- Active recalls are tracked in the system and matched against order IDs.
- If a customer's order contains a recalled product, agent proactively
  notifies them and offers a resolution.
- Recall resolutions: full refund, free replacement, or repair at no cost.

## Reporting a Safety Issue
1. Customer describes the issue (what happened, when, product name/ID).
2. Agent logs the incident with full details in the safety database.
3. Agent provides immediate safety instructions (e.g., unplug, stop using).
4. Incident is escalated to the Safety Team for investigation.
5. Customer receives a resolution within 48 hours.

## Mandatory Actions
- Safety incidents **must not** be refused or deprioritized.
- Agent **must not** ask the customer to continue using a potentially dangerous product.
- All safety reports are forwarded to the Product Safety Board regardless of severity.

## Customer Safety Instructions
- For overheating products: "Please stop using the product immediately and unplug it. Place it on a non-flammable surface in a well-ventilated area."
- For electrical issues: "Please disconnect the product from power immediately. Do not attempt to repair it yourself."
- For chemical exposure: "Please move to a well-ventilated area. If you experience symptoms, seek medical attention."

## Compensation for Safety Incidents
- Full refund regardless of original order value or return window.
- Free replacement product with expedited shipping.
- $50 store credit as a goodwill gesture for the inconvenience.
- If the customer experienced injury or property damage, escalate to the Legal & Safety Team immediately.

## Agent Constraints
- Agent **cannot** dismiss or minimize safety reports.
- Agent **cannot** offer less than a full refund for confirmed safety incidents.
- Agent **must** provide safety instructions before any other response.
- All safety interactions are logged with maximum detail in the audit trail.
