# Complaint Resolution Policy

## Acknowledgment
- All customer complaints must be acknowledged within **24 hours**.
- The agent provides a complaint reference number (CMPL-XXXXXXXX) upon acknowledgment.
- Customer receives confirmation via email with expected resolution timeline.

## Severity Classification
- **Low**: Minor inconvenience, no financial impact (e.g., late notification, website bug).
  Resolution target: 3 business days.
- **Medium**: Moderate impact, potential financial loss (e.g., delayed delivery, wrong item).
  Resolution target: 5 business days.
- **High**: Significant impact, safety concern, or legal threat (e.g., defective product causing injury, data breach).
  Resolution target: 24 hours with immediate escalation.

## Resolution Options
1. **Refund**: Full or partial, depending on the complaint severity and order value.
2. **Replacement**: Expedited shipping of a replacement item at no cost.
3. **Store Credit**: Bonus credit (110% of complaint value) as a goodwill gesture.
4. **Service Recovery**: Complimentary service upgrade, extended warranty, or loyalty bonus points.
5. **Escalation**: Transfer to a senior support specialist or management for complex cases.

## Escalation Triggers
- Customer mentions legal action, lawyers, or lawsuits
- Complaint involves personal injury or property damage
- Complaint has been unresolved for more than 10 business days
- Customer has filed a complaint with a regulatory body
- Repeated complaints from the same customer (3+ in 6 months)

## Compensation Guidelines
- **Low severity**: Up to $10 store credit or 50 bonus loyalty points.
- **Medium severity**: Up to $25 refund or replacement + 100 bonus loyalty points.
- **High severity**: Full refund, replacement, or up to $100 store credit + 500 bonus points.
- Anything above these limits requires manager approval via the Human Gate system.

## Follow-Up
- Customer satisfaction survey sent 48 hours after resolution.
- If customer is unsatisfied, case is reopened and escalated one severity level.
- Resolution quality is tracked in the agent's audit log.

## Agent Constraints
- Agent **cannot** issue compensation above the guidelines without human approval.
- Agent **must** log every complaint with full details in the audit trail.
- Agent **must** provide the complaint reference number to the customer.
