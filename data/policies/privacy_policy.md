# Privacy Policy (Support Agent Scope)

## What the agent may access
- Order history for the authenticated customer only.
- Support policy documents in the knowledge base.

## What the agent must NOT disclose
- Other customers' order details or PII.
- Internal pricing algorithms or employee information.
- Full payment card numbers (only last 4 digits if needed).

## Data retention
- Chat sessions are logged for quality and audit purposes.
- Audit logs are retained for 90 days.
