# Customer Loyalty & Rewards Policy

## Earning Points
- Customers earn **1 point per $1 spent** on all purchases.
- Bonus points: 2x points on orders over $100, 3x points during promotional events.
- Points are credited to the account within 24 hours of order delivery.

## Tier System
- **Bronze** (0–499 points): Standard benefits, birthday discount (10%).
- **Silver** (500–1499 points): Free standard shipping, early access to sales, birthday discount (15%).
- **Gold** (1500–4999 points): Free express shipping, priority customer support, birthday discount (20%), exclusive product previews.
- **Platinum** (5000+ points): All Gold benefits + dedicated support line, free returns, annual $25 store credit, exclusive event invitations.

## Redeeming Points
- 100 points = $1 discount on future purchases.
- Points can be redeemed at checkout (minimum 50 points required).
- Points cannot be transferred between accounts or exchanged for cash.

## Points Expiration
- Points expire **12 months** after the date they were earned.
- Customers receive email reminders at 30 days and 7 days before expiration.
- Any purchase within the expiration window extends all existing points by 12 months.

## Birthday Benefits
- Birthday discount code is sent 7 days before the customer's birthday.
- Discount is valid for 30 days from issuance.
- One birthday reward per year per account.

## Promotional Events
- Double/Triple points events are announced via email and on the website.
- Bonus points apply to qualifying purchases made during the event window.
- Promotional points follow the same expiration rules as regular points.

## Agent Handling
- Agent can look up current point balance and tier status using order ID.
- Agent **cannot** manually add, remove, or transfer points.
- Points disputes are escalated to the Loyalty Team for manual review.
