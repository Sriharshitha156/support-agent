# Shipping Policy

## Standard Shipping
- Free shipping on orders over $35 USD.
- Delivery within 5–7 business days (domestic).

## Express Shipping
- Available for $14.99 flat rate.
- Delivery within 2 business days.

## Tracking
Customers can track orders using the order ID on our website or by asking
the support agent for a status update.

## Lost or Damaged Packages
Report within 14 days of expected delivery. Agent will initiate a carrier
investigation and offer replacement or refund per investigation outcome.
